SECTION 1: PLAN OVERVIEW
--------------------------
Plan Name        : Star Health Gold Elite
Plan Code        : SH-GOLD-2024
Policy Type      : Individual Health Insurance
Sum Insured      : ₹10,00,000 (Ten Lakhs Only)
Policy Tenure    : 1 Year (Renewable)
Free-Look Period : 15 days from date of receipt

SECTION 2: ELIGIBILITY
--------------------------
- Minimum Entry Age  : 18 years
- Maximum Entry Age  : 65 years
- Dependents Covered : Spouse, Children (up to age 25)

SECTION 3: ROOM RENT LIMITS [CLAUSE 3.1]
--------------------------
The insurer shall bear room rent charges subject to the following caps:

  3.1.1 — Standard / General Ward     : ₹2,000 per day (maximum)
  3.1.2 — Semi-Private Room           : ₹3,500 per day (maximum)
  3.1.3 — Private / Deluxe Room       : ₹5,000 per day (maximum)
  3.1.4 — ICU / Critical Care         : ₹7,500 per day (maximum)

IMPORTANT [CLAUSE 3.2 — OUT-OF-POCKET RULE]:
  Any room rent charged by the hospital in excess of the limits above
  shall be classified as "User Out-of-Pocket Expense" and will NOT be
  reimbursed by the insurer. The policyholder bears the difference in full.
  Example: If the billed rate is ₹9,000/day and the applicable limit is
  ₹5,000/day, the excess ₹4,000/day is an out-of-pocket expense.

SECTION 4: COVERED PROCEDURES AND DIAGNOSES [CLAUSE 4.0]
--------------------------
The following diagnoses and associated treatments are covered under this plan,
subject to sum insured limits and waiting period conditions:

  4.1 — Acute Myocardial Infarction
        ICD-10 Code  : I21.9
        Coverage     : Emergency hospitalisation, thrombolysis, angioplasty,
                       ICU charges, pharmacy, lab investigations (ECG, Troponin,
                       Echocardiogram), and post-discharge cardiac rehab (up to
                       ₹10,000 per policy year).
        Waiting Period: Nil (emergency condition; no waiting period applies).

  4.2 — Appendectomy (Appendix Removal)
        ICD-10 Code  : K35.8
        Coverage     : Surgical procedure, OT charges, anaesthesia, post-op
                       care, standard ward stay up to 5 days.
        Waiting Period: 30 days from policy inception.

  4.3 — Coronary Artery Bypass Graft (CABG)
        ICD-10 Code  : Z95.1
        Coverage     : Surgical, OT, ICU, post-op rehab.
        Waiting Period: Nil (emergency condition).

SECTION 5: EXCLUSIONS [CLAUSE 5.0]
--------------------------
The following are NOT covered under this policy under any circumstances:

  5.1 — Cosmetic and Aesthetic Treatments
        Any surgery or treatment performed for cosmetic, aesthetic, or
        reconstructive purposes unless resulting directly from an accident
        covered under this policy. ICD-10 range: Z41.1 – Z41.9.

  5.2 — Pre-Existing Conditions (Waiting Period — 24 Months) [CLAUSE 5.2]
        Any illness, disease, or condition that existed, was diagnosed, or
        was treated within 24 months prior to the policy inception date is
        excluded. After 24 consecutive months of uninterrupted coverage,
        pre-existing conditions are eligible for coverage.

  5.3 — Self-Inflicted Injuries
        Treatment arising from intentional self-harm or suicide attempts.

  5.4 — Substance Abuse
        Treatment for alcoholism, drug dependency, or substance abuse disorders.

  5.5 — Experimental Treatments
        Any treatment not recognised by the Medical Council of India (MCI)
        or that lacks documented clinical efficacy.

SECTION 6: CLAIMS PROCEDURE [CLAUSE 6.0]
--------------------------
  6.1 — Cashless Facility: Available at network hospitals. Pre-authorisation
        required within 4 hours of admission (emergency) or 48 hours prior
        (planned).
  6.2 — Reimbursement Claims: Submit within 30 days of discharge. Required
        documents: original bills, discharge summary, investigation reports,
        prescription copies, and policy number.
  6.3 — TPA Helpline: 1800-XXX-XXXX (toll-free, 24×7).
